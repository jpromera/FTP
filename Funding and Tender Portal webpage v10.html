Companies at a glance

One row per company (entities merged by normalised legal name). Coordinator and Participates in count distinct projects; Participations is the raw number of award lines. Click any company to open its full profile. Use the instrument, type-of-action and country filters plus the search below to narrow the list — money and project counts then reflect only the matching participations.

Methodology & sources
⚠️
This dashboard reflects the public projects index at extract time. Very recently signed grants may lag. Triangulate any material figure against the individual project page before quoting it externally.

Source & methodology

SourceEU Funding & Tenders Portal — public SEDIA search index (no login). European Commission · EU Funding & Tenders Portal
Extract date29 May 2026
InstrumentsEuropean Defence Fund (EDF), Act in Support of Ammunition Production (ASAP) and the European Defence Industry Reinforcement through common Procurement Act (EDIRPA). Instrument is derived from the call identifier (EDF-ASAP-* → ASAP; EDF-EDIRPA-* → EDIRPA; otherwise EDF).
Not includedPADR (2017–2019) and EDIDP (2019–2020) predecessor programmes are not published in this public projects index and are therefore absent by design.
EU contributionPer-participant value from API field eucontribution; project total from euContributionAmount. Figures in euro.
EU funding rateHeadline rate is the portfolio ratio (total EU contribution ÷ total overall budget). Per-project rate is shown where the source reports it; 147 lump-sum projects carry no per-project rate.
Country filterApplies to coordinating country for projects and to entity country for participants. The "contribution by country" chart distributes funding to each participating entity's country.
Contact dataParticipant telephone and e-mail are never exposed by the public API.
Source: EU Funding & Tenders Portal (public SEDIA index) · extract 29 May 2026. Built in the EU Defence Funding reference style. Dashboard v10 · 29 May 2026 · JPR
